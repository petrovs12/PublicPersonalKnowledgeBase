Minimum Viable Product

Definition: A Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is the most minimally featured thing you can build that will address the Opportunity for most of the Target Customers .

MVP should be focused, very good at what it does, and not doing too much!!!

Sometimes, First MVP should be very hacky, human powered and not automated (e.g. solved-by-hand instances.)
