Green's Theorem

This gives the relationship between line integral of a simple loop and a double integral of a 2D region in a plane the sipmle loop encircles.

Note to self:

The circle on the integral denotes that we traverse the thing clockwise. Interestingly, as the integral symbol+ the dxdxdx notation together determine what the 'sum' is taken over, we can expect some tricky notations there.
