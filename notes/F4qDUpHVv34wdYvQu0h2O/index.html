SVD

SVD is in a sense a generalization of eigenvalue decomposition, but can be applied to non-square matrices.

More concretely, if the singular values are σ1,σ2,...,sigman\sigma_1,\sigma_2,...,sigma_nσ1​,σ2​,...,sigman​, then σi2\sigma_i^2σi2​ are the eigenvalues of A^T*A=A^2$ . SVD from wikipedia

Relationship between SVD and Linear Algebra:

Suppose that AAA has full column rank and let A=QRA=QRA=QR be a QRQRQR decomposition for A. There exists a SVD of A s.t. Q=UQ=UQ=U iff A∗AA*AA∗A is diagonal.
