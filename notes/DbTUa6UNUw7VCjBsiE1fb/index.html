StrassenAlgorithm

Strassen's algorithm is a divide-and-conquer algorithm for matrix multiplication. It works by noting that the product of two matrices of size n×nn\times nn×n can be expressed as follows:

XY=[ABCD]∗[EFGH]=[AE+BGAF+BHCE+DGCF+DH]XY = \begin{bmatrix} A & B \\ C & D \end{bmatrix}*\begin{bmatrix} E & F \\ G & H\\ \end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix} AE+BG & AF+BH \\ CE+DG & CF+DH \\ \end{bmatrix}XY=[AC​BD​]∗[EG​FH​]=[AE+BGCE+DG​AF+BHCF+DH​]

The running time will be then: T(N)=7T(n2)+O(N2)T(N ) = 7T(\frac{n}{2})+O(N^2)T(N)=7T(2n​)+O(N2) where the O(N2)O(N^2)O(N2) part comes from the fact that we have a bunch of N/2∗N/2N/2*N/2N/2∗N/2 matrices to sum (in order to combine).
